## Verified Hardware Facts (confirmed from physical photo fantom_08_3.jpg)

### Tone Category Buttons [1]–[16] — CONFIRMED LAYOUT
Single row of 16 buttons (NOT 2x8), left to right, numbered 1–16:
1: A. PIANO
2: E. PIANO
3: ORGAN
4: KEYS
5: GUITAR
6: BASS
7: STRINGS
8: BRASS
9: WIND
10: CHOIR
11: SYNTH
12: PAD
13: FX
14: DRUMS
15: USER
16: ASSIGN

IMPORTANT: The buttons are in ONE ROW of 16, not two rows of 8.
IMPORTANT: Button 13 is FX (not SFX). Button 14 is DRUMS (not Drum).
The labels printed on the panel are abbreviated (e.g., "A. PIANO" not "Ac.Piano").
